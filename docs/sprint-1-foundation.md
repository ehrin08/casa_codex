# Sprint 1 Foundation

This document tracks the current Sprint 1 foundation for the Casa Paraiso Spa Management System.

## Completed Foundation Items

- Laravel 12 project setup.
- Livewire, Tailwind CSS, Vite, MySQL, Git, and GitHub setup.
- Initial database schema, models, relationships, migrations, and seeders.
- Shared Blade layout at `resources/views/layouts/app.blade.php`.
- Placeholder pages for public, management, therapist, and customer areas.
- Route placeholders for `/`, `/management`, `/therapist`, and `/customer`.
- Fake development seed data for roles, services, users, profiles, and therapist availability.

## Placeholder Areas

- Public landing page: Sprint 1 system overview and links to role areas.
- Management area: future services, therapists, customers, appointments, transactions, reports, promotions, and reviews.
- Therapist area: future schedule, assigned appointments, commission summary, and customer notes.
- Customer area: future booking, appointments, services, promotions, and reviews.
